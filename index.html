    Methodology

    Data: CMIE Prowess quarterly financials, 30 large NSE/BSE-listed Indian firms, 1995-2014. Test set = 2012-2013.

    Target: Binary indicator of at least 150 bps EBITDA margin compression over next 2 quarters.

    Model: Tuned LightGBM (Optuna, 50 trials), 23 features including CPR, ICSI, IBD, WCSS, OL, SIB plus lag and momentum features.

    Limitations: 30-firm universe, ends 2014 (no post-pandemic), inventory/working-capital fields sparse (IBD and WCSS computable for less than 30% of rows), no macro features (WPI/Brent/INR-USD).
